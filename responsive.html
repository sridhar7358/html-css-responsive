MEET OUR DESIGNER

Tripura Sundari is a renowned fashion designer based in Chennai, India. With a passion for creating timeless and elegant designs, Tripura has established herself as a leading figure in the Indian fashion industry.

Tripura Sundari's journey began in 2006 when she pursued a Diploma in Fashion Designing at SCS Kothari Academy of Women. She further honed her skills by completing a Bachelor's degree in Fashion Design from Pearl Academy of Fashion in 2011.

Prior to launching her own label, Tripura gained valuable experience as a stylist for several prominent actors in the South Indian film industry. Her work with celebrities such as

Srikanth, Vikram Prabhu, Jeeva, Aarav,Santhosh Prathap, Ashok Selvan, Mirchi Shiva, Jagan, Ramya Pandian, Simran, Bindu Madhavi, Priya Mani, and Anjali
